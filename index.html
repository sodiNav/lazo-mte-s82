Slowly Sketch your way to your Greatest Work

Welcome to Palette Town—a curated, accessible hub designed for anyone who has ever wanted to pick up a pencil or paint a canvas but didn't know where to start. Art can feel overwhelming with all of its rules, techniques, and terminology. Palette Town cuts through the noise by organizing the essential building blocks of drawing, painting, and color theory into easy-to-navigate districts. Think of us as your friendly local guide on your creative journey.

Digital Art

Software workflows, layer management, tablet configuration, and digital brush techniques.

Traditional Art

Pencil sketching, ink work, acrylic/oil/watercolor techniques, and surface textures.

Studio Fundamentals

Color theory, perspective, light and shadow, and anatomical proportions.
